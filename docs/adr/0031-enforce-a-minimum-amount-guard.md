# ADR 0031: Enforce a minimum amount guard

- Status: Accepted
- Deciders: arisu6804

## Context

The YieldVault smart contract needs a clear, documented approach to "enforce a minimum amount guard" so the codebase stays consistent and auditable.

## Decision

We enforce a minimum amount guard as the standard for this contract, in line with Soroban best practices.

## Consequences

Improves clarity, testability, and maintainability, and gives future contributors a recorded rationale to build on.
